About I-Stem

Founded by people with disabilities with lived experience and decades of accessibility, engineering, and AI experience, I-Stem strongly believes in the power of technology and community to create equitable experiences and opportunities for all. Through our range of individual and enterprise products, services, and initiatives, we seek to empower people with disabilities and support organizations in better catering to their students, employees, or customers with disabilities.
